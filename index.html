Tails Spin

Tailspin is action adventure animated series about a bear, Named Baloo Von Bruinwald XIII who is a cargo pilot flying throughout the fictional town of Cape Suzette in the 1930s. Along his journey as a contract pilot he faces many different characters to include sky pirates, a rich and evil arch nemesis, and other perilous challenges, below you will find a list of characters

  • Baloo von Bruinwald XIII
  • Rebaecca Cunningham
  • Kit Cloudkicker
  • Don Karnage
  • Shere Khan

Fun facts

It features characters adapted from Disney's 1967 animated feature the Jungle Book


It aired on the Disney Channel On September 7th, 1990 and ended on its 65th episode on August 8th, 1991


The show had three different TalesSpin Video Games for the Gameboy, Sega Genesis, and Nintendo Entertainment System

Where Dreams come from

My name is Eugene Washington, I am watched Talespin as much as I could when I was younger and thoroughly enjoyed the show, so much so that 30 years after its airing I still watch it on Disney+ with my son today. Today aside from being a web developer and father, I am also a pilot. I want to credit Talespin for the inspiration it gave me at such a young age. To many it is just a cartoon but I can honestly say (with no credit taken away from my parents) that a cartoon gave me some very early direction, because of a group of bears flying seaplanes around mountains, dodging crazy weather, fighting sky pirates, racing robot pilots and other crazy villainous animals it gave me a dream and something to stick to. 30 years later I am here catching a tailwind because TailSpin!
